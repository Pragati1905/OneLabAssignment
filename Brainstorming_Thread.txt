Goal: Identify mismatches between platform records and bank records.

Assumptions:
- Platform records transactions instantly
- Bank settles transactions with delay (1–2 days or next month)
- Matching is based on transaction_id

Possible mismatch types:
1. Timing mismatch (month difference)
2. Amount mismatch (rounding errors)
3. Missing transactions (platform but not bank)
4. Extra transactions (bank but not platform)
5. Duplicate entries (bank side)

Approach:
- Load both datasets
- Match using transaction_id
- Compare amount and date
- Identify unmatched records
- Flag discrepancies with reason
